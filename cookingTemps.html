Proper cooking temperatures for certain food groups and the time internal temperature must be maintained

Food safety cooking temperatures for their respective foods according to FDA Food Code 2022. Further information and specifics can be found in the FDA Food Code 2022 release on the FDA's website.
Food/Food group Cooking Temperature Time
Intact meats, fish, eggs 145°F ≥15 seconds
Ground Meats 155°F ≥17 seconds
Poultry 165°F >1 second
